Formula of TF-IDF:

TF-IDF (Score ) = TF x IDF

Ex : (hello)
		= TF(hello) x IDF (hello)
		= 0.1667 x 2.3219
TF-IDF(hello)	= 0.387060
 



Determination of TF :

TF = Freqency Term/ [Maximum it apper in any document]

Ex : hello

TF (hello) 	= (hello) word occur in particular article / Maximum no of (hello) word occur in hole articles 
		= 5/ 30
		= 1/6
		= 0.1667



Determination of IDF :

IDF  =  log (N / nT )

Ex: hello

IDF (hello)  	= log ( No of articles/ How much no of artciles contain word (hello))
		= log(100/20)
		= log(5)
		= 2.3219
